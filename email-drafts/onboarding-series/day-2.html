Your landing page might be working against you

Hi,

You have landing pages, pitch decks, and one-pagers already live. But here's the thing nobody tells you: most marketing assets slowly drift away from your actual positioning. The deck a designer polished six months ago might be telling a story your strategy has moved past.

PMM Sherpa audits your existing assets against proven frameworks: Dunford's positioning test, Miller's StoryBrand structure, Hormozi's value equation. It reads your live URL, pulls competitor pages for benchmarking, and tells you exactly what to fix. Not vague "improve the copy" advice. Specific rewrites with the reasoning behind each one.

Three prompts to try now

Audit your assets

LANDING PAGE AUDIT

"Audit this landing page: [paste URL]. Grade it against Dunford's positioning framework, StoryBrand, and Hormozi's value equation. Pull 2-3 competitor pages and show me where they're beating me. Give me the 5 most impactful rewrites."

Tip: Use your real production URL. Sherpa reads the live page via Jina Reader, so it sees exactly what your visitors see.

SALES DECK REVIEW

"Here's our sales deck [paste or describe the slides]. Audit it: Is the narrative structure clear? Does it follow a problem-solution-proof arc? Where does it lose the buyer? Give me slide-by-slide recommendations."

Tip: Copy-paste your slide text or describe each slide's content. Include your target buyer persona for a more focused critique.

ONE-PAGER CRITIQUE

"Review this one-pager for [product]. Our ICP is [describe them]. Does the messaging match what this buyer cares about? What's missing? Rewrite the sections that are weakest. Here's the text: [paste it]"

Tip: Always include who the asset is for. A one-pager for a CTO needs completely different emphasis than one for a VP of Marketing.
