Three prompts for career growth

Level up your career

INTERVIEW PREP

"I'm interviewing for a [title] role at a [type of company]. My background is [brief summary]. Give me: the top 5 questions the hiring manager will likely ask, a strong answer structure for each, and a positioning statement for myself as a candidate."

Tip: Include the job description if you have it. Sherpa will tailor the questions and answers to that specific role instead of giving generic PMM interview advice.

30/60/90 DAY PLAN

"Build me a 30/60/90 day plan for a [title] at [company type]. The product is [describe it]. The team is [size/structure]. Focus on quick wins that demonstrate PMM value to cross-functional stakeholders."

Tip: Mention what stage the company is at (early-stage, scaling, enterprise) and whether PMM is a new function or an existing team. This changes the plan significantly.

PROMOTION NARRATIVE

"I'm preparing for a promotion conversation from [current level] to [target level]. Here's what I've shipped this year: [list key projects]. Help me frame these accomplishments using PMM leadership frameworks. What's the strongest narrative I can tell?"

Tip: List your projects with outcomes (revenue impact, adoption numbers, stakeholder feedback). Concrete results make the narrative far more compelling.
