Three prompts for sales enablement

Build a competitive battlecard

"Build a competitive battlecard: us ([product]) vs. [competitor]. Include: their positioning, their strengths, their weaknesses, our differentiators, objection handling for their top 3 claims, and discovery questions that expose their gaps. Pull their current messaging from their website."

Tip: Name the specific competitor. Sherpa will pull their live site data and cross-reference it with what's in the knowledge base about their positioning strategy.

Create discovery questions

"Write 10 discovery questions our AEs should ask prospects who are also evaluating [competitor]. The questions should surface pain points where we win and expose areas where [competitor] is weak. Include the expected answer and our suggested follow-up for each."

Tip: Include your strongest differentiators and the competitor's known limitations. The more you tell Sherpa about the competitive landscape, the sharper the questions.

Handle a live objection

"A prospect just said: '[paste the exact objection].' They're comparing us to [competitor]. Write me a response that acknowledges their concern, reframes it, and pivots to our strength. Keep it conversational, something an AE can actually say on a call."

Tip: Paste the exact words the prospect used. Generic objections get generic responses. Real quotes get responses your AEs can use verbatim.
