It’s been a while since I updated the workbooks for Obviously Awesome and Sales Pitch. Today’s the day!

This is a freebie for **newsletter subscribers**. Because y’all are super special!

Here’s what’s new:

*   A revised 14-page Workbook that covers the Second Edition of Obviously Awesome and Sales Pitch

*   A new completed example Positioning Canvas

*   A new completed example Sales Pitch Canvas

*   A new example Sales Pitch PowerPoint with Notes

*   An updated and revised 12-page Messaging Document

There are three files - the workbook, the example PowerPoint, and the messaging doc. You can download them separately or together in a zip file.

**If you are a subscriber already, you should have received this newsletter from me directly in your inbox.** Click on the link in that email and you will go directly to Gumroad. A discount code is automatically applied and you can download the files for free.

If you are seeing this on the website and not in an email from me, then you will have to subscribe. When you subscribe, you will get a **welcome email**. That welcome email **will include a link to the free templates**. Hate email? No problem, you can unsubscribe any time you like and still keep the templates! It’s like literal magic. I don’t ask for much around here, folks, so deal with this one tiny ask.

If you are a subscriber and cannot find the email, please check your spam first (try searching your email for “April Dunford”). Spam filters are a thing!! If all else fails, comment here, and I will sort you out.

I hope these templates are useful! Let me know what you think!
