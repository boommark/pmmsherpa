As I mentioned in previous newsletters, I am releasing a second edition of my Positioning book, Obviously Awesome. It’s a fairly significant update to the original book. There’s a new pre-work section, a new chapter on value, added content for large companies, and a lot more. [You can find out more about what’s new in this post](https://aprildunford.substack.com/p/announcement-obviously-awesome-the).

While I grow old waiting for Amazon, **I’ve decided to give you - my beloved, smart, and good-looking newsletter subscribers - early access to the book**. Woo hoo!! Here’s a secret - the new book is available on Amazon today, but only if you know how to look for it.

Confusion over the old vs new paperback is what is stopping me from officially launching this book for everyone today. Follow these steps to get to the new paperback edition.

**Step 1** - Go to your local Amazon site - yes, every country has its own Amazon

**Step 2** - search for “Obviously Awesome April Dunford” and click on the orange book in the results. **Make sure you select the paperback version from the list of formats on the right-hand side.**(Paperback links for [USA](https://www.amazon.com/Obviously-Awesome-Product-Positioning-Customers/dp/1999023056/ref=monarch_sidesheet_title), [Canada](https://www.amazon.ca/Obviously-Awesome-Product-Positioning-Customers/dp/1999023056), and the [UK](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Obviously-Awesome-Product-Positioning-Customers/dp/1999023056/ref=monarch_sidesheet_title)).

**Step 3** - If you do not see the yellow sticker on the cover, that is the old version of the book. **Click the link that says “See all formats and editions” as illustrated below.**
