## The Market We're Defining

**Why the old "conversation intelligence" definition no longer fits**

The analyst community defined this category in 2018–2020 as "call recording + transcription + coaching." That definition served a world where sales tech was fragmented. That world is gone.

**Our thesis — three claims we're prepared to defend:**

**1. Revenue intelligence is collapsing into the GTM data layer.**
Standalone RI tools are becoming features. The competitive question is: which data platform is the substrate? ZoomInfo's contact and intent graph gives Chorus a compounding data advantage no pure-play RI vendor can replicate.

**2. Pre-call intelligence is the next frontier, not post-call analysis.**
Every RI vendor solves for "what happened on the call." The unsolved problem is "what should your rep know before hitting Join?" Chorus is the only platform with a native answer.

**3. The unit of value is shifting from recordings to revenue signals.**
CROs don't want more call summaries. They want earlier warning on deal risk and faster pattern recognition across their book. The category needs a new evaluation framework that reflects this.

## Competitive Positioning

**How we see the competitive landscape — honestly**

The Sales Intelligence and Revenue Intelligence categories are converging. Vendors are sorting into two camps: (1) point tools with deep feature sets but isolated data, and (2) platform layers with broader data but historically shallower RI features. Chorus sits at the intersection.

**2×2: Data Depth vs. RI Feature Depth**

| | Shallow RI Features | Deep RI Features |
|---|---|---|
| **Rich Data Layer** | *ZoomInfo (pre-Chorus)* | **Chorus by ZoomInfo** ← our position |
| **Thin Data Layer** | *Niche recorders (Fireflies, tl;dv)* | *Gong, Salesloft* |

**Our moat — three layers:**

- **Data moat:** ZoomInfo's contact graph (300M+ contacts, 100M+ companies) is the largest in B2B. This data compounds into Chorus's deal intelligence in ways competitors cannot replicate without building or acquiring an equivalent data asset.
- **Integration moat:** Native integration across ZoomInfo's full platform (SalesOS, MarketingOS, OperationsOS) creates switching costs that go well beyond the RI tool itself.
- **Motion moat:** A ZoomInfo contract renewal is also a Chorus renewal conversation — channel economics no standalone RI vendor can match.
