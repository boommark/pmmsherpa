## What Didn't Work

Honest misses — root causes and what we'd change.

**1. Analyst Relations — Missed Gartner MQ window**
*What happened:* The category brief draft started 6 weeks late due to competing launch priorities. We submitted a preliminary positioning document but couldn't complete the full MQ submission in time.
*Root cause:* No dedicated AR owner; analyst work is squeezed between launches. PMM team of 4 can't absorb AR as a side motion at this ARR.
*What we'd do differently:* Hire a dedicated AR/PR contractor for H2. Set hard internal deadlines 10 weeks before submission, not 4.

**2. Mid-Market Webinar Series — Low pipeline conversion**
*What happened:* 3 webinars, avg. 214 registrants, 89 attendees — but only 6 net-new MQLs total. Strong content, weak follow-up handoff to SDR team.
*Root cause:* No SLA between PMM and SDR for webinar lead follow-up. Leads sat in HubSpot for avg. 9 days before first touch.
*What we'd do differently:* Define 24-hour follow-up SLA with SDR leadership before next event. Build automated trigger sequence from webinar platform → HubSpot → SDR queue.

**3. Developer Persona Messaging — Too broad**
*What happened:* API v3 launch copy targeted "developers" as a monolith. Resonated poorly with Staff+ engineers evaluating for enterprise; resonated better with indie builders.
*Root cause:* No developer persona split in our ICP framework. We assumed one audience, got two very different responses.
*What we'd do differently:* Split developer persona into 3 sub-segments before Q3 launches. Run 5 customer interviews per segment first.
