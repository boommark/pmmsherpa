# PMM Context

Drop this file in the root of your project (or the folder you work from with
Claude / ChatGPT). PMM Sherpa will read it before drafting or critiquing, and
will treat it as authoritative ground truth — your voice wins over Sherpa's
defaults.

All sections are optional. Delete what you don't need. Replace bracketed
prompts with your own content.

## Product

[One paragraph: what the product does, who it's for, the core problem it
solves, and the wedge that makes it different. Plain language, no marketing
voice yet.]

## Audience

[ICP shape: company size, industry, tech stack signals. Buyer persona: title,
seniority, daily pains, what they currently use, what they hate about it.
Include the "do nothing" alternative if it's a real competitor.]

## Voice

[Three to five short rules. Examples: "We use second person." "We avoid
'platform', 'solution', 'leverage'." "Sentences under 18 words." Add one or
two short before/after examples if you have them.]

## Positioning

[Your current positioning statement, if one exists. Even if it's rough or you
think it's wrong — Sherpa needs to see the starting point.]

## Competitors

[Named competitors and the alternatives buyers actually consider, including
homegrown spreadsheets and "do nothing". One line per competitor on how the
buyer perceives them.]

## Prior artifacts

[Links or paths to existing brand assets, decks, canonical landing pages,
internal narrative docs, or anything else that captures how you already talk
about the product.]
